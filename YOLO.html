Real Time Face Recognition

Well my dream is to have a personal assistant whom I can boss around :p. Well many of them have this dream
But I am really working for it. First we would need a human face detector. Though it sounds easy it is not.
Our cameras have this feature but they usually miss out some faces and detect faces only if kept at a certain
angle. I am trying to build a multi-view face detector. Also after that we are going to try and learn those
faces and store them so that we can record the voices of the person and using that detect the person's speech
Also we would require to make a system which detects who is talking based on visual and audio inputs.
It is a big project most likely to be going to go on for my entire B.Tech period but a worthy project :).
