Improving Voice Separation by Incorporating End-to-End Speech Recognition

Last Updated: 26th April

Recent work in speech separation works good in constrained environments but still perform poorely in noisy backgrounds. To further improve voice separation in noisy environments we propose using transfer learning from Automatic Speech Recognition Models. We extract deep features from an End-to-End Automatic Speech Recognition model trained on a completely different dataset, LibriSpeech, and use that in a 2-Stage separation model. The experimental results thus obtained are promising. For understanding the paper please visit the Medium Article. For in-depth understanding you can have a look at the paper on IEEE or ARXIV
